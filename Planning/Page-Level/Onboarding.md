# Onboarding — Page-Level Planning

## Page Purpose
First-run experience for new users. Guides them from sign-up to their first tangible artifact (avatar card, draft script, or video preview). Not a permanent sidebar page — it's a one-time flow that leads into the main app.

## Key Objects / Entities
- Welcome step (goal selection, niche, content ambition)
- Theme selection (accent color — optional during onboarding)
- Avatar creation (from scratch or from Discover template — Discover scope TBD, see Avatars.md open questions)
- First artifact generation (script or short video preview)
- Workspace layout orientation

## Target Metrics
- Time-to-first-avatar: < 5 minutes from sign-up (Go-To-Market §4)
- Time-to-first-publish: < 30 minutes for short-form (Go-To-Market §4)
- Onboarding completion rate (User-Research §5)
- Drop-off step tracking
